Camp Fee:

The camp fee of $300 is due with the application. The actual cost to support you camper for a week is over $600. Please consider contributing more than the required fee if you are able.

If you are unable to pay $300 now, please pay $150 now and $150 on the first day of camp, or call Colleen at (630) 690-0944 as a few scholarships are available.
